My name is Hsien-Yu Meng(孟宪妤 in simplified chinese and 孟憲妤 in traditional chinese). I am currently a MSc student in Dept. of Computer Science, Tsinghua University. Before that, I obtained BEng from Dept. of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University.

I was a research engineer in a start-up company,Owlii, which aims to build the holographic platform, from Jan. 2016 to Mar. 2018. This is our realtime demo in SIGGRAPH 2017. My work consists of mesh deformation and facial animation. One of our work was conditionally accepted by SIGGRAPH ASIA 2018 (Video to Fully Automatic 3D Hair Model, supervised by Linda G. Shapiro and Ira Kemelmacher-Shlizerman).

I did research on point cloud segmentation algorithm, advised by Lin Gao, Yu-Kun Lai, and Dinesh Manocha. Our result outperforms the state-of-the-art by 30% in terms of Mean IoU on S3DIS indoor semantic segmentation dataset. Part of our work is submitted to CVPR 2019 19(under review) and we will keep improving it and launch another submission to CVPR 19.

My research interests are in Computer Graphics, including pointcloud segmentation, non-rigid registration and facial performance capture.
