My name is Hsien-Yu Meng(孟宪妤 in simplified chinese and 孟憲妤 in traditional chinese). I am currently a MSc student in Dept. of Computer Science, Tsinghua University. Before that, I obtained my bachelor's degree from Dept. of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University.

I was a research engineer in Owlii, which aims to build the holographic platform, from Jan. 2016 to Mar. 2018. My work consists of mesh deformation and facial animation. One of our work was conditionally accepted by SIGGRAPH ASIA 2018 (Video to Fully Automatic 3D Hair Model, supervised by Linda G. Shapiro and Ira Kemelmacher-Shlizerman).

During this summer, I did research on point cloud segmentation algorithm, advised by Lin Gao, Yu-Kun Lai, and Dinesh Manocha. Part of our work was submitted to AAAI 19(under review) and we will keep improving it and launch another submission to CVPR 19.

My research interests are in Computer Graphics, including pointcloud segmentation, non-rigid registration and facial performance capture.
